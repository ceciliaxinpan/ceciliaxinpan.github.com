About me

"Be the change you wish to see in the world."

I am in the doctoral program in Learning Technologies at University of Texas at Austin. I am originally from China and studied translation for economics and trade during her undergraduate in Beijing. Before coming to UT, I studied Digital Media and Education at University of Michigan-Ann Arbor and graduated in December, 2015. My interests in educational technology include interface design, game-based learning, and online learning. I have been working as a web developer on campus for 2 years. I love animals and has a cat and a puppy at home and enjoy hiking and swimming.
